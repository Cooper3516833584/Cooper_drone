# First low-altitude flight checklist

1. Choose an open, controlled test area with legal permission to fly.
2. Assign pilot, observer, and companion-computer operator roles.
3. Confirm battery health, charge level, and secure mounting.
4. Confirm GPS, EKF, and Home position readiness.
5. Confirm RC manual takeover works before autonomous actions.
6. Confirm kill switch behavior.
7. Confirm configured altitude limits.
8. Use an initial takeoff altitude around 1 m.
9. Keep hover duration short.
10. Save and review logs after the test.
